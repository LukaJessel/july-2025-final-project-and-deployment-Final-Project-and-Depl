About Clinical Supplies

We are dedicated to providing high-quality clinical and medical supplies to healthcare professionals, clinics, and hospitals. Our mission is to ensure reliable access to essential products that support patient care and safety.

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Clinical Supplies has grown from a small local distributor to a leading supplier of medical equipment and consumables across Kenya and East Africa. Our commitment to quality and service has earned us the trust of hundreds of healthcare providers.
